What is an infection

A pathogen is a micro organism that causes diseases, the pathogens will invade the body. The two main pathogens are bacteria and viruses, bacteria can only be killed by antibiotics.Viruses on the other hand can not be killed but treated with vaccines.

Pathogen:Ebola

Ebola is a virus that can be transmitted trough close or direct contact with infected bodily fluids.This virus was first identified in Africa near the ebola river where the virus takes its name from. The shape of this virus is round and long, closely resembles a worm.This virus will mainly taget your cells and immune system, it will kill your cells then explode them. The incubation period of this virus is 21 days, some symptoms you may experience is pain in the abdomen, chest, joints or muscles. You may also experience coughing up blood, diahreea or vomit.
